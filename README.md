## 🌟 Premium Features

🛡️ **Military-Grade Security (HWID Locked)**  
Every instance of OMT-Sentinel is tied to the user's specific Hardware ID (Motherboard UUID). Your license cannot be stolen or used on unauthorized machines.

⚔️ **True Multi-Account Separation**  
Safely decouple your "Catcher" and your "Spammer" accounts to drastically reduce detection risks. One account spawns, the other catches.

🧠 **Intelligent Captcha & Incense Management**  
The moment a Captcha drops, Sentinel pauses your fleet and dynamically issues `inc p` (pause) in your active channels. Resume seamlessly with a remote `!done` to automatically blast `inc r` (resume).

⚡ **Blazing Fast Auto-Catcher & Solver**  
Advanced asynchronous logic guarantees millisecond-precision catching. It features a built-in regex-powered hint solver backed by a complete custom-built offline database of 2000+ Pokémon forms.

📱 **Remote Controller Infrastructure**  
Control your entire fleet from your smartphone or main account. Send commands like `!pause`, `!done`, and `!stop` remotely via Discord without needing to look at the host PC. 

✨ **Legendary & Shiny Tracking**  
Built-in radar tracks and immediately logs encounters with Legendaries, Mythicals, Ultra Beasts, and Paradox Pokémon directly to your private Discord log channel.

📊 **Live Terminal HUD**  
A beautiful, real-time terminal display tracks your exact catch speed, shiny count, captcha encounters, and total catch count.
